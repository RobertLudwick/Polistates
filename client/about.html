Polistates

This project is currently being developed by the following students: Bobby Ludwick, Ian Davis, Manny Quinones, Griffin Bortnick, and Sean Larson.
The goal of this project is to create a game to assist in the learning process of students in an International Relations class. The game will allow students
to control states and choose how they want to interact with other states on the map. As students play, they will be presented with real world scenarios that will prompt
them to choose how they want to handle each scenario. Students will also have to manage their resources for their state by controlling tiles on the map, which have
different resources on each tile.

As of now, the project is still in it's building phase, and we are working towards having a testable product in the coming weeks. If you are interested in testing
our product, please go to the "Beta Testing" page and fill out the respective form.
